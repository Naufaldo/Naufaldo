
# Hi there 👋

I'm **Naufaldo, M.Sc.**, a Cold Storage & Automation Engineer, Robotics Researcher, and Director of **CV Dingin Lestari Teknik** based in Bandung, Indonesia.

My background combines industrial engineering and academic research, with experience in refrigeration systems, cold storage engineering, industrial automation, control systems, and mobile robotics.

I earned my Master of Science in Mechatronics Engineering from National Taipei University of Technology (Taipei Tech), Taiwan. My research focused on intelligent control strategies for mobile robotics, multi-agent systems, robot formation control, and autonomous navigation.
